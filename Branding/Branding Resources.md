# Tools

1. [Moby Thesaurus: A literary thesaurus with more useful word links](https://moby-thesaurus.org/)
1. [Rhymezone: Rhyming words](www.rhymezone.com)
1. [Linguee: International translator/dictionary that is way more sophisticated than google translate](https://www.linguee.com/)
1. [Onelook: Loosely related words thesaurus](www.onelook.com/thesaurus)
1. [Wordsafety: Make sure your name isn't offensive](http://wordsafety.com)
1. [WIPO: Search trademarks internationally](www.wipo.int/branddb/en/index.jsp)
1. [Australian trademark search](https://search.ipaustralia.gov.au/trademarks/search/quick)
1. [IWantMyName: Domain name finder](https://iwantmyname.com/)
1. [NameMesh: Generate multiple domain names](https://www.namemesh.com/)
1. [Word Nerd: Combines moby with definitions and cloud](https://wordnerd.fun/relief)
