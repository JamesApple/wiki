# Tools

1. [Moby Thesaurus: A literary thesaurus with more useful word links](https://moby-thesaurus.org/)
2. [Rhymezone: Rhyming words](https://github.com/JamesApple/wiki/tree/ee251b40e8bb4b63c8dcea6f612c70777307e016/Branding/www.rhymezone.com)
3. [Linguee: International translator/dictionary that is way more sophisticated than google translate](https://www.linguee.com/)
4. [Onelook: Loosely related words thesaurus](https://github.com/JamesApple/wiki/tree/ee251b40e8bb4b63c8dcea6f612c70777307e016/Branding/www.onelook.com/thesaurus/README.md)
5. [Wordsafety: Make sure your name isn't offensive](http://wordsafety.com)
6. [WIPO: Search trademarks internationally](https://github.com/JamesApple/wiki/tree/ee251b40e8bb4b63c8dcea6f612c70777307e016/Branding/www.wipo.int/branddb/en/index.jsp)
7. [Australian trademark search](https://search.ipaustralia.gov.au/trademarks/search/quick)
8. [IWantMyName: Domain name finder](https://iwantmyname.com/)
9. [NameMesh: Generate multiple domain names](https://www.namemesh.com/)
10. [Word Nerd: Combines moby with definitions and cloud](https://wordnerd.fun/relief)
